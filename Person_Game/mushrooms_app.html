ROBOTICUTE

Mushroom Adventure

Roboticute is a small creature from Robot Planet. Usually he descovers space on his own spaceship. Now he just landed on Mushroom Planet. He received an SOS message from Princesses on that planet months ago. His trip was very long and energy tank is empty. Roboticute knows how to convert mushrooms into the energy. Help him to restore energy level and pass his mission.

Roboticute: Use keyboard's arrows UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT to move Roboticute.
Use key P or button Pause Game to pause or resume game.

Mushrooms: There are two types of mushrooms at the Planet: Boroviks and Amanitas. They are growing and (if nobody picks it up) dying. Roboticute can pick up mushrooms to raise an energy level. Borovik will increase green energy, Amanita – red energy. Roboticute uses 1 red and 1 green energy unit periodically.

Frogs: Collision with any frog will convert it to Princess.
Be careful – Princesses are very beautiful and slowly increase level of green energy , but they consume a lot of red energy. If number of Princesses more than 4 – Roboticute begin to use one more green energy unit (or even two units). You can consider red energy as money, green energy as food. No money – no Princesses. They will leave you when red energy level becomes zero.
